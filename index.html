Welcome to Bob Wallet

Bob Wallet was created to help preserve Bitcoins fungibility. Today it is easy to trace bitcoin transactions from address to address by simply using any public Block Explorer such as blockchair, which will give you information about the general parameters of popular currencies: bitcoin, ethereum or dogecoin (like dogecoin stock symbol); but also detailed information about blockchain metrics. Bob Wallet helps fix this.
